Gangplank lives in RU and OCE

In all other regions expect for RU and OCE gangplank had a 0% play rate. This is probably because of him being disabled for the start of the event. For RU gangplank also had the highest pick rate for the region at 29.7%.

Every one hates Katarina

Katarina was the most banned champion in 8 of the 10 regions, TR seems to have hated her the most with a 82.1% ban rate.

The best items never bought

Players seemed oblivious to the new teleport boots enchantment as only RU and OCE players build these enchantments. With around 60% winrates for these enchantments it seems the other regions missed out.

No one loves Morello(nomicon)

Out of all the 10 regions with 10,000 games each Morellonomicon was never bought.
