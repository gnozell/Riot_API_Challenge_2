No one loves Morello(nomicon)

With 10,000 games surveyed in each of the 10 regions, it was discovered that the Morellonomicon item was never bought.

The best items never bought

Players seemed oblivious to the new teleport boots enchantment as only RU and OCE players built those enchantments. With around 60% win rates for the enchantments it seems the other regions may have missed out.

Gangplank lives in RU and OCE

In all regions except for RU and OCE, the champion: Gangplank had a 0% pick rate. This is most probably due to him being disabled at the start of the event. In the RU region, Gangplank had the highest pick rate at 29.7%.

Every one hates Katarina

Katarina was the most banned champion in 8 of the 10 regions. TR seems to have hated her the most with an 82.1% ban rate.
